ScanForge

ScanForge is a simple and powerful tool that helps you work with PDFs directly from your device. You can merge PDFs, compress documents, and convert images into PDF files easily.

Features

  • Merge multiple PDF files
  • Compress large PDFs
  • Convert images to PDF
  • Fast and simple interface
