Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ScanForge respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not collect or store personal information on external servers. However, the app may access the following on your device:

  • Photos – when you choose images to convert into PDF
  • Files – when you select documents for PDF processing

All files are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

2. Analytics and Crash Reporting

The app uses Firebase Crashlytics to help identify and fix application crashes. Crash reports may include limited technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Application version
  • Crash logs

This information does not personally identify you.

3. Data Storage

All files created or processed by the app are stored locally on your device. The developer does not have access to these files.

4. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party service:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – used for crash reporting

5. Data Sharing

No personal data or user files are sold, shared, or transmitted to external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page.
